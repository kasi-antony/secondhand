About Second Hand

We're on a mission to make second-hand the first choice — and renting the smart choice.

Second Hand was born out of a simple frustration: why buy something you only need for a week? Whether it's an iPad for a college project, a DSLR for a wedding shoot, or camping gear for one trip — we believe ownership shouldn't be the only option. We connect sellers with buyers and renters, creating a circular economy that's good for your wallet and the planet.
